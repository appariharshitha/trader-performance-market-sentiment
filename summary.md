## Approach
The analysis combines Bitcoin market sentiment data with trader-level transaction data from Hyperliquid to evaluate how Fear and Greed influence trading behavior and outcomes.

## Key Findings
- Trader PnL and win rates are lower and more volatile during Fear periods.
- Greed sentiment is associated with increased leverage usage, higher trade frequency, and a stronger long bias.
- High-leverage traders are significantly more affected by negative sentiment compared to low-leverage traders.

## Actionable Insights
- Limit leverage exposure during Fear sentiment to reduce losses.
- Encourage higher activity only for experienced traders during Greed periods.
- Prioritize consistent performers for capital allocation across all market conditions.

## Takeaway
Incorporating sentiment-aware controls and trader segmentation can improve trading performance and risk management.
